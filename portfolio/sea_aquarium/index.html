MAP GUIDE

EXPLORE S.E.A.

EXPLORE S.E.A. has a
total of 6 exploration areas.
Don't miss any of the zones and
have fun exploring every zone.
An amazing adventure awaits you.

Click on the map

1

  • 1 Tropical Zone
    Tropical Zone is a relaxing end to a journey through all the regions of the world at S.E.A. Aquarium. These tropical coral reef exhibits are appropriately arranged like a gallery of living art, where you’ll encounter curious garden eels popping up out of the sand.

    In Tropical Diver, living corals and thousands of colorful reef fish are presented in a faithful recreation of a tropical Pacific coral reef, complete with an overhead crashing wave.
    ×
  • 2
    Ocean Voyager
    Our Ocean Voyager gallery, built by The Home Depot is home to whale sharks, manta rays and thousands of other fascinating fish.

    As one of the largest single aquatic exhibits in the world, Ocean Voyager features an acrylic tunnel for guests to view thousands of marine creatures on all sides, as well as a giant acrylic viewing window to explore our oceans like never before.
    ×
  • 3 Dolphin Coast
    Cruise on over to S.E.A. Aquarium’s captivating coast, Dolphin Coast! This gallery features the Aquarium’s own pod of common bottlenose dolphins. Guests can admire the dolphins in both the Dolphin Coast lobby, as well as in the dolphin theater.

    The featured dolphin presentation is full of fun marine facts, a live training demonstration and educates guests about the Aquarium’s efforts in preserving this extraordinary species.
    ×
  • 4
    Beaver Dam
    This is a village where beavers built a dam. and Rivers are a source of life for animals, people and cultures throughout the world. In the Southern Company River Scout gallery, you’ll explore the wide diversity of animals found in the rivers of Africa, South America, Asia and right here in Georgia.

    This gallery is a themed environment full of waterfalls, logjams and a simulated meandering North American river that flows over your head.
    ×
  • 5
    Sea Lion Theater
    Our beloved California sea lions have returned! Truist Pier 225’s live presentation, Under the Boardwalk, features S.E.A. Aquarium’s incredible California sea lions.

    These highly social pinnipeds and their dedicated trainers will show you how they work together — and why this is such an important part of animal care at S.E.A. Aquarium — in a live training session.
    ×
  • 6
    Shark !
    S.E.A. Aquarium’s newest gallery, Sharks! Predators of the Deep®, is one of the largest and most dynamic shark exhibits in North America.

    Home to some of the most recognizable apex predators in the ocean, this exciting new gallery experience brings you face to face with the most enigmatic and misunderstood creatures in the sea. S.E.A. focus is to provide a fresh perspective on the vital role sharks play in the ocean.
    ×
